WP:NEWS
For current news in the world, see Portal:Current events and Wikipedia:News sources. For the what Wikipedia is not policy, see WP:NOTNEWS. For the "In the news" section of Wikipedia's main page, see Wikipedia:In the news.
There are various sources of news about Wikipedia, The Wikimedia Foundation that supports the project and other related Wikimedia projects.

Wikipedia
The Signpost (Shortcut: WP:POST, RSS). The Wikipedia community newspaper that is written by volunteers and published weekly.
Wikipedia:Milestones (Shortcut: WP:ANN, RSS). Important Wikipedia milestones.
Wikipedia:Goings-on (Shortcut: WP:GO). Weekly news and updates about existing or new projects or initiatives of Wikipedians.
Wikipedia:Administrators' newsletter (Shortcut: WP:ANEWS). Monthly newsletter containing topics of interest to administrators.
Wikinews:Category:Wikipedia, archives of news about Wikipedia
News aggregators: Google News · Techmeme
